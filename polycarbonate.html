Polycarbonate

Polycarbonate

Polycarbonate sheets offer the highest levels of glazing performance where transparency or tailored light transmission along with high impact strength is of vital importance. Their versatility makes them the ideal choice for glazing in hospitals, schools, sporting arenas and public infrastructure projects. They are available in transparent (compact sheets) & textured (emboss sheets) finishes.
