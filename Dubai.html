Welcome to Emirates Panel Manufacture LLC (EPM).

Why Choose Us

Emirates Panel Manufacture LLC (EPM) is located in New Industrial Area 1 Umm Al Qumain,United Arab Emirates the central of Middle East. We are specialised in manufacturing Aluminium Composite Panels,Coated Coils,Protective Film and Polycarbonate Sheets in Various colours. EPM was certified with ISO 9001:2015 Certification for complying with the International Quality Standards and performing Qaulity Management Systems.


The factory has the Annual Output of 60 Million Square Feet(5.5 Million Square Metre) of Aluminum Composite Panels and Coated Coils with actual accuracy and most importantly in a cost effective manner. Today EPM is one of the leading and most modern Aluminium Composite Panels and Coated Coils Manufacturing Factory in Middle East and the Whole of Africa.
